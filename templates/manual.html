How the ShiftSync Algorithm Works

1. Priority Logic

The algorithm assigns shifts based on a strict priority system:

  • Fairness First: It ensures no single engineer works significantly more shifts than anyone else.
  • Sat/Sun Balance: If an engineer works 2 or more shifts, it attempts to give them at least one Saturday and one Sunday.
  • 7-Day Rule: It actively avoids scheduling an engineer on a Sunday and the immediately following Saturday.
  • Preferences: It tries to assign engineers to the dates they ranked highest in their availability list.

2. Leave Blockouts

If an engineer is added to the Leave Blockouts list on the Availability page, the algorithm will absolutely ignore them for that date, even if they accidentally submitted it in their preferences.
